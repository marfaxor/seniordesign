Content Creator Story

Actor: Meghan - Content Creator

Opens browser and navigates to home url/admin which brings her to an admin login page

She enters a username and password and is directed to the employee portal page.

This page is populated with messages she has written up and the dates they will be sent to users, as well as a "create new message" button

When she clicks "Create new message", she gets a JS popup which allows her to enter a message that will fit in a text message and a date selector
that allows her to select the date this message will be sent. NOTE: Future release may include a "send right now" option.
