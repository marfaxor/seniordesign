Super Admin

Actor: Austin Wynn, owner of Never Alone Recovery

Austin opens his web browser and navigates to betterdays/admin, where the admin login fields appear.

Upon submitting credentials, Austin is brought to employee portal with:
    -Total number of active users and total number of help requests
    -Manage employees
        -Add new employee
            -name
            -contact
            -priority [low - med -high]
            -login information:
                -username
                -password
        -remove employee
    -Records
        -list of all tickets [timestamped] and who responded to them [also timestamped]
    -Sign up user
        -name
        -phone number
